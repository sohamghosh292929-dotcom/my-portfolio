About Us

At Colourstreak, design meets strategy. Founded in 2013, we blend 50+ years of experience to craft striking branding, packaging, and digital experiences trusted by startups and world-renowned brands.
Colourstreak offers expert solutions in podcast design, digital marketing, website, and application design, while also excelling in offline printing services such as signage, flex, brochures, flyers, brand identity, and custom merchandise like t-shirts.
